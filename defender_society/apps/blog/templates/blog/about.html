Mission Statement

To connect former Bigs, in Kentuckiana, to Big Brothers Big Sisters of Kentuckiana in order to support the long-term sustainability of the organization.

This will be accomplished by The Defender Society recruiting new adults to volunteer as Bigs, cultivating donors, and advocating for the organization.

Vision Statement

Enhance Big Brothers Big Sisters of Kentuckiana’s long-term ability to ensure that all youth achieve their full potential.

The Defender Society Activities

Professional Development Workshops

Potential workshops include: Leadership Development, Networking Skills, Conflict Management Skills, & Diversity, Equity and Inclusion training.

Alumni Leadership Giving Circle

Alumni are expected to donate a minimum of $1,000 annually to Big Brothers Big Sisters of Kentuckiana.

Benefits may include: special invitations to virtual and in-person events (post-pandemic), special recognition in the annual report, and Big Brothers Big Sisters of Kentuckiana logo gifts/items.
