Tool description:

  • This tool can check the status of friendship links in batches, and check whether the friend links added by the webmaster still have backlinks to their own website, so that the webmaster can delete and adjust invalid links
  • First fill in the information that needs to be detected (usually your website address) in the form below, and then fill in the links of the friend chain in batches, and click "start detection" to wait for the results to return
  • Note: The friendship link may not be placed on the homepage of the friend site, so be sure to fill in the link of the page where the friend link is placed on the friend site, otherwise it will be judged that no friend link has been added
Note: The detected information is generally the address that you provide to your friends site, that is, the address of your website
Note: The friend links of the friend site may not always be on the homepage, and the link is provided wherever the friend link is placed
